About

Dr. Robert Treharne is a Senior Lectuer in the School of Biosciences, University of Liverpool, UK. He specialises in teaching coding skills to anyone who wants to learn. He has a particular interest in the Canvas LMS and has developed a number of tools and resources to help educators and students get the most out of the platform. He has a PhD in Physics from Durham University.
